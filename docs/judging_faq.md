# Judging FAQ

## Why is this agentic and not just a dashboard?

The system detects events, replans, applies actions to the digital twin, and logs explainable decisions.

## Why not let the LLM decide everything?

Core business logic is deterministic for reliability and auditability. Language-model use is reserved for rationale and planning support.

## What is the core innovation?

The decision policy engine and mode switching between normal and crisis behavior.
